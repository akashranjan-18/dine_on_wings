About Us

Welcome to Dine On Wings

Welcome to Dine On Wings! Our story is a blend of love and passion for food, led by a mother and son duo. The mother, a gifted cook and wife of a retired Air Force officer, has infused her exceptional taste into every dish. Her son, passionate about food and leaving his IT career in Bangalore, joined her to share this culinary journey.

We believe that serving affordable, delicious food is a noble pursuit. From intimate dinners to grand celebrations, our party hall is perfect for any special occasion, starting at just ₹250 per plate. Let us make your event memorable with great food and heartfelt service.
